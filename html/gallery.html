I was born in Addis Ababa in a public health station around 4 killo. My dad, Asrate kassa and mom Yeshiwork simegn were both, at that time, teachers with low income. I already had 2 elder sisters, Tsigemariam and sebenemariam but only one was staying with us in Addis. I wasn’t a very healthy child. I was almost regularly sick and kept returning my parents to clinics. Watching my elder sister go to school and learn, I was very eager to join so I got into a nursery class at Abunegorgorios School at the age of 3. I learnt there until grade 12. I lived in a lot of neighborhoods while growing up but most of my memorable childhood was at ferensay legasion.

Educational background I learned in Abunegorgorios School from kindergarten up to 12th grade. I was an excellent student although a slight decrement was observed in the latter years.

For higher education I joined Addis Ababa University and chose engineering. I am now almost half way in pursuing an undergraduate degree in Information technology and engineering. University has its ups and downs but I say I’m having a good time and trying to gather as much information and knowledge as possible while contributing what I can to different parties and improve myself as a person.

ADDIS ABABA 5 KILO
